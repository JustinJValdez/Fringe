Jennie Baptista

Owner & Operator

I'm Jennie Baptista and I've been a licensed cosmetologist for over 35 years. I specialize in haircutting and perms, however, I love to do color and bleaching techniques as well, especially color formulation and color matching. I've been fortunate enough to experience many different facets that this AMAZING and sometimes INTIMIDATING industry has to offer.
With the help from my career councelor in high school, I was able to get the Cosmetology program added onto our R.O.P. high school curriculum, and took my cosmetology course my jr. and sr. year in high school. I managed my first hair salon at 18 years old where I grew not only as a stylist but as a true professional and realized it's all about the connection you make with your client. I moved on to working a commission position then went to the next obvious level of salon ownership and I currently hold my position as an Educator which is where I truly found my passion. I'm happiest when I'm teaching my techniques!

MISSION STATEMENT
